What is human trafficking?

Defined

Human trafficking is a modern-day form of slavery involving the illegal trade of people for exploitation or commercial gain.

Surely not in Utah?

Utah is in the top 10 states for reported human trafficking cases. We must work together to protect our community.

Who are the victims?

Men, women, and children of all ages have been victims of human trafficking. Approximately one fifth of all identified victims are children.

NHTH

The National Human Trafficking Hotline works with law enforcement and others to help victims here in Utah.

Facts about human trafficking in the United States

How many people are affected in the United States?

In 2021, 10,359 situations of human trafficking were reported to the U.S. National Human Trafficking Hotline involving 16,554 individual victims.

Who are the victims?

Out of all the victims, 64% are unknown, 28% are known minors and 9% are adults.

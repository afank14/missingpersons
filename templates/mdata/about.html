Blue Campaign - Homeland Security

Blue Campaign is a national public awareness campaign designed to educate the public, law enforcement, and other industry partners to recognize the indicators of human trafficking, and how to appropriately respond to possible cases. Homeland Security has resources for all to learn about human trafficking and discover what they can do to help. Visit Homeland Security's Website for more information.
